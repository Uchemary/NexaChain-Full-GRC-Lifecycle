# NexaChain-Full-GRC-Lifecycle
End-to-end GRC lifecycle engagement for NexaChain Technologies Ltd, a fictional UK crypto exchange. Covers governance review, risk assessment, compliance assessment, AI governance, and executive reporting. Frameworks: ISO 27001, NIST CSF 2.0, UK GDPR, MiCA, EU AI Act.

## Project Overview
This project demonstrates a complete end-to-end GRC (Governance, Risk and Compliance) 
analyst engagement for NexaChain Technologies Ltd, a fictional UK-regulated crypto 
exchange headquartered in London.

Unlike isolated GRC deliverables, this project follows the full lifecycle of a real 
GRC engagement, from initial scoping through to executive reporting, simulating 
exactly how a GRC analyst would work.

Every deliverable in this project was built independently by the analyst without 
AI generation, demonstrating genuine GRC knowledge and analytical capability.

## Key Findings Summary (Phase 3 — Governance Review)
- **12 findings identified** across governance, compliance and operational areas
- **5 Critical findings** requiring immediate remediation by August 2026
- **6 High findings** requiring remediation by September 2026
- **1 Medium finding** requiring remediation by October 2026

Critical findings include: No Incident Response Plan, No Risk Register, 
No CISO or Security Lead, No Business Continuity Plan, No DPO appointed.
